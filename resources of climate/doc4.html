Self Development Goals

1. No Poverty

Goal:

End poverty in all its forms everywhere.

Description:

This goal aims to eradicate extreme poverty, ensure equal access to resources and basic services, and promote social protection measures for all, particularly vulnerable populations.

2. Zero Hunger

Goal:

End hunger, achieve food security, and promote sustainable agriculture.

Description:

The focus is on ensuring that everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food, promoting sustainable agricultural practices, and improving food production systems.

3. Good Health and Well-Being

Goal:

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

Description:

This goal emphasizes the need to improve healthcare access, reduce maternal and child mortality, combat diseases, and promote mental health and well-being.

4. Quality Education

Goal:

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Description:

The aim is to provide free, quality primary and secondary education for all, improve access to vocational training, and promote education that fosters sustainable development.

5. Gender Equality

Goal:

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

Description:

This goal focuses on eliminating discrimination, violence, and harmful practices against women and girls, ensuring equal participation in leadership and decision-making.

6. Clean Water and Sanitation

Goal:

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Description:

The goal aims to provide universal access to safe and affordable drinking water, adequate sanitation facilities, and promote sustainable water resource management.

7. Affordable and Clean Energy

Goal:

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.

Description:

This goal promotes the use of renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and access to clean energy for all communities, particularly in developing countries.

8. Decent Work and Economic Growth

Goal:

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Description:

The aim is to foster economic growth while ensuring fair labor practices, decent working conditions, and opportunities for employment and entrepreneurship.

9. Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Goal:

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.

Description:

This goal focuses on developing quality infrastructure, promoting sustainable industries, and encouraging research and innovation to boost economic growth.

10. Reduced Inequality

Goal:

Reduce inequality within and among countries.

Description:

The aim is to address disparities in income and wealth, promote social, economic, and political inclusion, and ensure equal opportunities for all.

11. Sustainable Cities and Communities

Goal:

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Description:

This goal emphasizes the need for sustainable urban planning, reducing environmental impact, and improving infrastructure and public transport systems.

12. Responsible Consumption and Production

Goal:

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

Description:

The aim is to promote resource efficiency, reduce waste generation, and encourage sustainable practices in production and consumption.

13. Climate Action

Goal:

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Description:

This goal calls for integrating climate change measures into national policies, improving education and awareness, and enhancing resilience to climate-related hazards.

14. Life Below Water

Goal:

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources.

Description:

The aim is to protect marine ecosystems, reduce pollution, and address the impacts of climate change on marine life and habitats.

15. Life on Land

Goal:

Protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

Description:

This goal focuses on conserving biodiversity, managing forests sustainably, and combating desertification and land degradation.

16. Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Goal:

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable institutions.

Description:

The aim is to reduce violence, ensure equal access to justice, and promote accountable and transparent governance at all levels.

17. Partnerships for the Goals

Goal:

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize global partnerships for sustainable development.

Description:

This goal emphasizes the importance of collaboration between governments, the private sector, and civil society to mobilize resources and share knowledge for achieving the SDGs.
